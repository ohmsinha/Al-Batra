## What ISO 50001 brings

A documented framework for measuring, monitoring and improving energy performance — distinct from environmental management (ISO 14001) in that the focus is energy use, energy consumption and energy efficiency.

## Our implementation

- **Energy review** — significant energy uses, baselines, drivers
- **Energy performance indicators (EnPIs)** — defined, measured, reported
- **Operational controls** — for significant energy uses
- **Procurement controls** — for energy-consuming equipment and services
- **Internal audit and management review**
- **Stage 1 and Stage 2 attendance**

ISO 50001 is frequently combined with ISO 14001 for an integrated environment-and-energy management system.
