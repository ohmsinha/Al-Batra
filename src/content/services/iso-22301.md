## ISO 22301 in a UAE context

UAE financial regulators, government tenders and large-buyer supplier programmes increasingly expect a documented business continuity management system. ISO 22301 is the standard most commonly named.

## Our implementation

- **Business impact analysis (BIA)** — recovery time objectives, recovery point objectives, minimum business continuity objectives
- **Threat and risk assessment**
- **Recovery strategies** — for critical activities, supporting resources and dependencies
- **Business continuity plans** — for the activities the BIA identifies as in scope
- **Exercise programme** — desktop walk-throughs through to full simulation
- **Internal audit and management review**
- **Stage 1 and Stage 2 attendance**
