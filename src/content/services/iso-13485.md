## ISO 13485 in the UAE

UAE medical device regulation is shared between the Ministry of Health and Prevention (MOHAP) and emirate-level authorities (DHA in Dubai, DOH in Abu Dhabi). ISO 13485 is the de-facto quality system expected of any manufacturer or distributor — it forms the basis for product registration, supplier qualification and post-market surveillance.

## Our implementation

- **Design and development controls** — for manufacturers in scope
- **Risk management integration** — alignment with ISO 14971
- **Document and record control** — to the level the standard requires
- **Production and service controls** — process validation, traceability
- **Post-market surveillance** — complaint handling, advisory notices, vigilance reporting
- **Regulatory file** — supporting documentation for product registration

Al Batra implements ISO 13485 as the management standard; product registration and regulatory affairs work is delivered in coordination with a regulatory consultant where required.
