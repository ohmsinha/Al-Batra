## What an ISMS actually is

An ISO 27001 management system is not a technical security product. It is a documented way of identifying the information assets that matter, the risks against them, the controls applied, and how the whole thing is monitored and reviewed. The technical security controls live in your IT and operational stack — the ISMS gives you the structure to manage them.

## A pragmatic ISMS for a UAE professional-services firm

A well-scoped ISMS for an organisation of fifty to two hundred people is achievable in ten to fourteen weeks, with a Statement of Applicability sized to the actual operation rather than every control in Annex A. We treat the standard as a defensible audit framework — not a checklist to maximise.

## Our ISO 27001 implementation

- **Scoping** — ISMS boundary, in-scope assets and supporting services.
- **Risk assessment** — methodology, asset register, threat–vulnerability mapping, risk treatment.
- **Statement of Applicability** — Annex A controls, with justification for inclusion or exclusion.
- **Documentation** — information security policy, supporting procedures, technical and organisational measures.
- **Operational controls** — access management, change control, supplier management, incident response.
- **Internal audit and management review** — full cycle, findings logged, corrective actions tracked.
- **Stage 1 and Stage 2 attendance.**
