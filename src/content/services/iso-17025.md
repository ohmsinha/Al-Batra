## What ISO 17025 covers

ISO 17025 sets out the general requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories. Unlike a certification (ISO 9001-style), ISO 17025 results in accreditation by a recognised accreditation body — typically EIAC or ENAS in the UAE — and the scope of accreditation is specific to named tests or calibrations.
