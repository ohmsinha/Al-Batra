## Where ISO 22000 fits

In the UAE, food safety is regulated municipally — Dubai Municipality, Sharjah Municipality, Abu Dhabi Agriculture and Food Safety Authority and others each issue their own food safety regulations. ISO 22000 provides an internationally recognised framework that satisfies most municipal requirements when correctly mapped to local rules, and is increasingly demanded by international hotel chains, large catering contracts and export markets.

## Our ISO 22000 implementation

- **HACCP study** — hazard analysis and critical control point identification, with a defensible methodology auditors expect.
- **Prerequisite programmes (PRPs)** — cleaning and disinfection, pest control, supplier approval, traceability, allergen management.
- **GHP documentation** — good hygiene practice procedures aligned with Sharjah Municipality (or applicable emirate) requirements.
- **Food defence and food fraud** — registers and controls.
- **Operational PRPs** — for hazards that need control beyond CCPs.
- **Verification programme** — internal audit, microbiological monitoring and management review.
