## What ISO 45001 asks of you

ISO 45001 requires a documented occupational health and safety management system — hazards identified, risks assessed and controlled, workers consulted, incidents investigated, and the system reviewed by top management. It applies to every UAE workplace, but the implementation effort varies sharply by sector.

## Sector-specific implementation

- **Construction** — site-specific risk assessments, contractor management, permit-to-work, lifting plans, and the integration with DM/MOI/MOHRE notifications.
- **Manufacturing** — process hazard analysis, machine safety, chemical management, ergonomics.
- **Hospitality** — kitchen safety, slips and falls, evacuation planning across multiple operating areas.
- **Facilities management** — multi-site control of subcontractors, working at height, energy isolation.
