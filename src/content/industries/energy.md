## Where we work

- Upstream and midstream oil and gas operators
- Renewables developers — solar EPC, project operators
- Water utilities, water treatment, desalination
- Waste management and recycling operators

## Standards that typically apply

- **ISO 50001** — energy management, with energy reviews, EnPIs and operational controls
- **ISO 14001** — environmental management, aspect–impact registers and legal compliance evaluation
- **ISO 45001** — occupational health and safety, particularly for plant and field operations

## How we engage

Energy engagements often run alongside an internal sustainability programme — corporate reporting, scope 1/2/3 disclosure or alignment to emirate-level efficiency targets. We do not replace those programmes; we make sure the management system gives them a defensible operational foundation.
