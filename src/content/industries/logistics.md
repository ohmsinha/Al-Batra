## Where we work

- Freight forwarders operating sea, air and land freight
- Third-party logistics operators (3PLs)
- Free-zone trade operators (Jebel Ali, KIZAD, Hamriyah, DAFZA and others)
- Cold-chain logistics for food and pharma

## Standards that typically apply

- **ISO 9001** — for tender qualification, customer audits and group quality policy
- **ISO 28000** — supply chain security, increasingly required by large shippers and freight forwarder networks
- **ISO 14001** — for environmental management, especially for cold-chain and last-mile fleets
- **ISO 45001** — for warehouse and yard safety

## How we engage

Logistics engagements often cover several operating sites under a single certificate scope. We map the management system to your operational network — the documentation reflects what each site does, not a single generic warehouse procedure. Multi-site internal audits and surveillance audits are planned and budgeted up front.
