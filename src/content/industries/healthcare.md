## Where we work

- Outpatient clinics and day-care surgical centres
- Diagnostic laboratories — clinical pathology, microbiology, molecular
- Medical device manufacturers and distributors
- Testing and calibration laboratories supporting healthcare operators

## Standards that typically apply

- **ISO 13485** — quality management for medical device manufacturers and distributors
- **ISO 15189** — medical laboratories
- **ISO 17025** — testing and calibration laboratories
- **ISO 9001** — for clinics, group quality policy and tender qualification

## How we engage

Healthcare engagements are aligned to the regulatory authority your operation is licensed under — DHA, DOH or MOHAP — and the certification body (or accreditation body, for ISO 15189 and ISO 17025) you intend to work with. Documentation is built to satisfy both, with no duplication.
