## Where we work

- Law firms, advisory firms, accounting practices
- Financial services groups regulated by the SCA, CBUAE, DFSA or FSRA
- IT operators — managed services, SaaS, cloud platforms
- Consultancies and design practices

## Standards that typically apply

- **ISO 27001** — information security management, scoped to firm size and the data actually handled
- **ISO 22301** — business continuity, often required by financial regulators or large client contracts
- **ISO 9001** — for quality management, particularly where international group policy applies

## How we engage

Professional-services engagements emphasise scope discipline. The Statement of Applicability for ISO 27001, the recovery time objectives for ISO 22301 and the process scope for ISO 9001 are negotiated against the actual operation — not the largest possible documentation set. The aim is a system the firm can run with one nominated owner, not a documentation library that requires a dedicated team.
