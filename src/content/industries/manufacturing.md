## Where we work

- Process plants — chemicals, plastics, metals, building materials
- Packaging lines — flexible packaging, rigid packaging, label converters
- Industrial fabricators — metal fabrication, modular construction, MEP fabrication
- Tier-1 and tier-2 automotive and electrical suppliers

## The certificates that typically apply

- **ISO 9001** — for tender prequalification, OEM supplier programmes and group quality policy
- **ISO 14001** — for environmental impact, aspect–impact registers and legal compliance evaluation
- **ISO 45001** — for occupational health and safety, with process hazard analysis and machine safety
- **ISO 50001** — for energy management, particularly for energy-intensive process operations

## How we engage

Manufacturing engagements are almost always integrated — quality, environment, safety and (where relevant) energy in a single management system. Documentation lives on the plant network or on a document control platform you already operate, not in our hands. Internal auditors are nominated from your team and trained as part of the engagement.

## Specifics worth flagging

- **Process realities first** — procedures reflect the actual flow of work, line by line, shift by shift. We do not lift templates.
- **Bilingual where it matters** — operator instructions on the line are routinely issued in both English and Arabic.
- **Free-zone alignment** — for clients in Jebel Ali, Hamriyah, KIZAD, RAKEZ and others, certification is mapped to free-zone HSE requirements where they overlap.
